Description

This project focuses on designing and testing affordable, customizable 3D-printed prosthetic limbs. The goal is to improve accessibility for people in underserved communities by using 3D printing to reduce cost and speed up production while keeping the prosthetic lightweight and functional.

The plan includes researching existing 3D-printed prosthetic designs, modeling designs in CAD, printing prototypes, and evaluating strength, comfort, and usability.

Skills Learned

  • Research and literature review
  • CAD-style iterative design workflow
  • Prototyping and evaluating design tradeoffs
  • Collecting feedback and improving a design
  • Project planning with constraints
