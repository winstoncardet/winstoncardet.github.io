Description

This project analyzes the 2021 T-Mobile breach as a cybersecurity case study. It explains how the breach happened, what data was exposed, how many people were affected, and what actions were taken after discovery (including investigation, identity protection offers, and legal/financial outcomes).

The paper also highlights broader lessons about risk, weak security configurations, the impact of stolen personal data, and why continuous security improvement and monitoring matter.

Skills Learned

  • Security incident analysis and summarizing technical events clearly
  • Evaluating impact on users and organizations (identity theft risk, trust, legal consequences)
  • Understanding attacker motivations and the stolen-data market
  • Research writing with sources and references
  • Drawing cybersecurity best-practice takeaways from real incidents
